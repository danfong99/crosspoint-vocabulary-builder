Dictionary Prototype

Interactive prototype simulating dictionary lookup on the Xteink X4 with Crosspoint firmware.

Word Selection

1. Double-tap CONFIRM to activate dictionary mode
2. Navigate lines with VOL ▲ VOL ▼
3. Move between words with LEFT RIGHT
4. Press CONFIRM to look up selected word
5. Double-tap CONFIRM to exit dictionary mode

Definition View

· LEFT RIGHT page through long definitions
· VOL ▲ VOL ▼ browse related words
· CONFIRM look up selected related word or add to vocab
· BACK close definition
